Get the official reMARK app.

reMARK is an enterprise email solution for teams that work with high volume email on shared email addresses.

With this app you have access to emails, contacts, search and mailing lists from your iPhone and iPad. 

App functionality:
- Send, receive, reply and forward emails
- Browse through folders in emails, contacts and mailing lists
- Add favourite folders
- Get notifications on specific folders
- Mark emails as read/unread
- File to folders and work-tray from emails and contacts
- Comment 
- View actions on emails and contacts
- Assign categories to emails
- Download attachments 
- Use templates
- View contacts with all details from reMARK
- Filter through emails
- Use advanced search in emails, contacts and mailing lists
- Work without internet connection
